Word Scramble

Word Scramble is an against the clock word game where you solve increasingly longer anagrams before the timer runs out.

How to Play

In Word Scramble, you simply solve the anagrams before the time runs out. The quicker you solve them the more points you get.

In round one you will have 10 seconds to solve a four letter anagram. How many seconds left on the timer is the points you will receive.

Each subsequent round the timer will increase by five seconds and the anagram will increase by one letter.

The last anagram will be a ten letter word and 40 seconds.

Points will accumulate each round for a final score total.

All words must be in the English dictionary and no names, places, brands, abbreviations, or anything that would normally be capitalised.
